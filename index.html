Global Clicker Web by nix_acuted

Welcome stranger! The goal is simple: click to earn points and see how high you can score. As you accumulate points, you'll unlock new skills to help you earn even more points effortlessly. One exciting skill you can unlock is Automatic Counting, which adds points to your score automatically every 0.4 seconds. To activate this feature, you'll need to reach a total of 120 points. The second skill available is the click multiplier, increased by 6 more points with each click but with a limitation of 1 minute for activity and being available every 1500 points.
